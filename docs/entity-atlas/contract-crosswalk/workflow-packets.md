# Workflow packets contract crosswalk

This page helps front-desk leads, general managers, care/message reviewers, and regional operators see which entity facts move through each automation workflow, what the app may only draft or recommend, what a human must review, and where labor-saving outcomes are captured. Example: a manager can see that a grooming rebooking suggestion depends on a staff-verified checkout and contact permission; the packet may draft outreach, but it cannot send a message, change the booking calendar, or apply a discount without review.

Markdown here is a navigation and evidence crosswalk. The behavioral authority remains the linked `domain`, `app`, `storage`, and integration source/Rustdoc/tests.

Crosswalk back-links: use [surface inventory](surface-inventory.md) for source/Rustdoc/test evidence, [source/provider flows](source-provider-flows.md) for provider entry and normalization, [storage/persistence](storage-persistence.md#workflow-packets-agents-and-app-runtime-entities) for persisted outcome/projection evidence, [runtime exposure](runtime-exposure.md#exposure-table) for API/worker/CLI/web/script surfaces, and the [operator workflow index](../../workflows/operator/README.md) or [entity index proof paths](../../design/entity-index.md#contract-crosswalk-proof-paths) to return to non-coder navigation.

## Shared packet pattern

All shipped packet surfaces follow the same safety shape:

1. Source or staff evidence enters through domain values such as `domain::source::{Provenance, RecordRef}`, `domain::workflow::{Event, PolicyContext}`, `domain::policy::ReviewGate`, and resort entities in `domain::entities`.
2. The `app` workflow builds a typed request/evaluation packet with source refs, review gates, safe actions, blocked actions, and optional draft artifacts.
3. An agent may summarize, rank, classify, validate, or draft only inside the packet contract. An `AgentPromptPacket` is evidence plus policy, not live authority.
4. Human or system-of-record review remains required for customer sends, provider/PMS writes, booking/check-in/out changes, payments/refunds/discounts, schedules, source hiding, medical/behavior/safety decisions, personnel actions, and policy exceptions.
5. Outcome records, audit drafts, approval records, or storage projections capture what staff did, whether the recommendation helped, and what labor minutes or manual-work category were saved.

## Packet relationships that matter

- Manager daily brief is the aggregator: it can consume checkout packets, retention packets, service-demand facts, data-quality issues, labor snapshots, and prior outcomes, then create source-grounded ranked manager actions.
- Checkout completion gates retention: a retention/rebooking packet should not become eligible unless checkout evidence is staff-verified and source-grounded.
- Data-quality hygiene guards every workflow: missing, stale, duplicate, conflicting, ambiguous, or sensitive facts should become hygiene candidates before they drive customer-facing drafts or provider-facing work.
- Daily updates are message drafts, not sends: included/omitted facts, internal flags, approval records, and blocked send stubs are part of the packet, not optional notes.
- Regional exceptions are planned: cite manager/data-quality outcomes and operations/portfolio vocabulary as feeders, but do not describe a shipped regional app workflow until a dedicated packet, tests, and outcome storage exist.

## Current evidence gaps to preserve

- Booking triage, checkout completion, CRM retention, and daily update workflows have app/test evidence but not dedicated durable outcome storage like manager daily brief and data-quality hygiene.
- CRM retention has an app `OutcomeRecord`, but no `storage::operations::CrmRetentionOutcomeRecord` in the inspected storage surface.
- Daily update/Pawgress has a draft-only MVP and send stub, but no proven live channel integration or writing-time outcome store.
- Regional labor exceptions is a future portfolio view; no dedicated app module, agent spec, tests, or regional outcome store exists yet.
- None of these packets authorize live Gingr/PMS writes, customer sends, schedule mutations, payment movement, policy/safety approvals, or source-data hiding by themselves.
