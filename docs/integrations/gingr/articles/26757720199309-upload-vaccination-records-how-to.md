# Upload Vaccination Records (How-To)

Source: https://support.gingrapp.com/hc/en-us/articles/26757720199309-Upload-Vaccination-Records-How-To
Section ID: 25381181698317

In this article, we will show how you can easily submit your pet's immunization records from the Customer Portal by simply dragging and dropping a scanned PDF or image file of their records.

 Video Tour

 Upload Immunization Records in the Customer Portal 

 Log in to the Customer Portal and click on the left-hand paw print Account button or scroll down on the home page and select the paw print Account button.

 Select the up-arrow cloud icon Upload Records .

 On the  Upload Records page, you can choose one of three options:
 
 Drag & drop the file onto the up-arrow cloud icon

 Select the up-arrow cloud icon and manually select your computer file

 Send an email to the designated @shots.pet email address.

 Once the file is uploaded, a green Success box with checkmark will appear.
