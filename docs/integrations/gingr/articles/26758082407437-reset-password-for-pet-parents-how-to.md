# Reset Password for Pet Parents (How-To)

Source: https://support.gingrapp.com/hc/en-us/articles/26758082407437-Reset-Password-for-Pet-Parents-How-To
Section ID: 25381181698317

Reset Password 

 Navigate to the customer portal for your facility, and select Forgot Password?

 Enter your email address and select Send Reset Link.

 Check your email inbox for the email containing the password reset link.

 Select the "set password" option. Once reset you can then log into the customer portal using your new password.
