NVA Pet Resorts · source-grounded AI workflow evidence

Safe AI workflows for reducing pet-resort labor cost

This repo demonstrates how a deterministic pet-resort app can give agents source-grounded context, validate reviewable drafts, block unsafe side effects, and capture labor-minute outcomes for managers and front-desk teams.

What this is

A safe automation foundation for pet-resort operations: source facts, workflow state, review gates, draft workflows, storage/audit records, and provider integration boundaries.

What it is not: a claim that agents can operate live NVA systems, message customers, change schedules, move money, or resolve ambiguous source facts without deterministic app approvals and human review.

Executive thesis

Labor cost falls when staff stop rediscovering the same source facts

Across a 170-site pet-resort operating context, repeated manager and front-desk work appears in morning dashboard reconciliation, checkout exception review, retention prioritization, source-data cleanup, and repetitive customer or staff drafts. The useful AI pattern is not a free-form chatbot. It is source-grounded workflow automation with review gates and measured outcomes.

The app owns source facts, workflow state, policy gates, audit trails, outcome capture, and every external side effect. Agents only summarize, rank, draft, and recommend from app-owned context packets.

Plain-language map

The repository models operating facts, source evidence, review gates, draft workflows, storage records, and integration boundaries so AI agents can help staff without owning business truth.

  • Pet-resort facts and policy explain what staff can trust, when facts are ambiguous, and what must stay reviewed.
  • Reviewable workflow packets explain what an agent may draft or recommend for a manager, front desk, or reviewer.
  • Outcome and audit records explain what work was completed, deferred, suppressed, or sent back as wrong-source cleanup.
  • Gingr/source-system boundaries explain how provider data becomes evidence before any workflow uses it.

Safety boundary

Rustdocs are evidence, not production authorization

Agents are review-gated and do not perform live member, customer, provider/PMS, schedule, payment, refund, discount, incident, vaccine, medical, safety, or personnel side effects without deterministic app-owned approval contracts and human review. Source ambiguity is preserved for review; it is never hidden by agent prose.
