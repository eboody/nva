# Service-line operations, capacity, assignments, and packages safety overlay

This overlay helps front-desk leads, care-team leads, groomers, trainers, general managers, and regional operators reduce repeated service-line lookup and exception-routing work for boarding, daycare, grooming, training, retail, capacity, assignments, packages, cancellation, minimum-stay, and service-line workflow actions. It shows what source facts automation may read, what it may draft or recommend, what a human must approve, and which outcome/audit record proves safe use.

Markdown here is an orientation layer. Source/Rustdoc/tests remain the behavioral authority; this page does not authorize autonomous live execution.

## 1. Plain-English entity/action definition and labor-cost problem

Service-line operations are the resort decisions that determine whether a pet can be boarded, checked into daycare, assigned to playgroup, booked for grooming or training, offered a package/upsell, or routed to retail/POS/reorder review. The costly manual work is scattered: staff compare provider service names, room or playgroup capacity, policy rules, pet care/temperament/vaccine facts, staff coverage, package/session balances, no-show and cancellation history, inventory, and source-quality notes before deciding what queue owns the next step.

Safe outcome: automation may assemble source-backed review packets, rank manager/front-desk queues, draft internal tasks or customer-message copy for approval, and record reviewed outcomes. It must not confirm, cancel, check in/out, assign live capacity/staff, book calendar slots, consume packages, move money, write to Gingr/PMS/POS, approve medical/behavior/vaccine/policy exceptions, or send customer messages.

## 3. Who/what is authoritative

| Fact or decision | Authoritative source | What it can prove | What it does not prove |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Provider/read-model service fact | `domain::source::{RecordRef, Provenance}` and integration/storage records where present. | Which upstream system, endpoint, record id, timestamp, schema, and payload backed a service-line recommendation. | Human approval, availability promise, provider/PMS/POS write permission, or policy truth. |
| Boarding capacity/deposit/cancellation/minimum-stay decision | `domain::boarding::*` modules and `domain::boarding::Contract`. | Semantic policy outcome such as available/waitlist/deny, deposit readiness, cancellation penalty, minimum nights, or upsell review reason. | Booking confirmation, cancellation execution, deposit collection/refund, room inventory mutation, or customer send. |
| Daycare eligibility/assignment/package decision | `domain::daycare::*` modules and `domain::daycare::Contract`. | Staff/manager/behavior/medical/customer-message review lane for daycare attendance, coverage, group play, assignment, incident, front-desk queue, or package opportunity. | Live admission, playgroup placement, package enrollment/consumption, payment movement, or incident reinstatement. |
| Grooming estimate/rebooking/reminder decision | `domain::grooming::*` and shared `domain::policy::ReviewGate`. | Duration estimate, care/history/no-show/rebooking/reminder review state, and customer-message gate. | Calendar booking, groomer assignment, discount/deposit application, or customer reminder send. |
| Training assignment/progress/package/follow-up decision | `domain::training::*` and shared policy gates. | Enrollment readiness, trainer/capacity waitlist/review, package ledger state, evidence-backed progress/outcome/follow-up readiness. | Live trainer assignment, package/payment adjustment, provider write, graduation claim approval, or parent-facing send. |
| Retail recommendation/POS/reorder decision | `domain::retail::*` plus provider retail DTO/mapping where present. | Saleability, inventory, recommendation, customer-copy review, POS review, or reorder task decision. | POS transaction, vendor order, payment/refund/discount, medical claim, or customer send. |
| Workflow packet and blocked actions | `app::manager_daily_brief`, `app::agents`, `domain::workflow`, `domain::policy`. | What may be ranked, summarized, drafted, validated, or recorded and which review gates/blocked actions apply. | Live execution in provider, calendar, payment, message, schedule, or source systems. |
| Outcome/value record | `app::manager_daily_brief::OutcomeRecord`, `storage::operations::ManagerDailyBriefOutcomeRecord`, workflow results. | Reviewed disposition, actor/persona, source refs, before/actual minutes, correlation id, and estimated/actual minutes saved. | Guaranteed future ROI or proof that the agent performed a live action. |
| Human approval | Role-specific approval record or workflow gate such as `ReviewGate::ManagerApproval`, `MedicalDocumentReview`, `BehaviorReview`, `CustomerMessageApproval`, `RefundOrDepositException`. | The named reviewer approved/rejected/escalated the sensitive step under that gate. | Approval for unrelated customer/provider/payment/schedule/safety/policy actions. |

## 4. Agent may read

Automation may read only source-backed, scoped facts carried by app packets, source refs, read models, storage projections, or domain values:

- Boarding: location, pet species, accommodation preference, room/capacity snapshot, care profile, feeding/medication readiness, deposit/payment status, cancellation notice, housekeeping/handoff/minimum-stay contract values, and upsell eligibility evidence.
- Daycare: service variant/care mode, attendance recurrence and exceptions, staffing roster snapshot, staff-to-pet ratio, temperament/vaccine/incident evidence, group-play eligibility evidence, playgroup capacity, front-desk readiness, package and payment state, and customer-message readiness.
- Grooming: service type, appointment duration evidence, breed/coat estimate, prior approved service history, style/care references, no-show/late-cancel history, rebooking cadence, reminder consent/approval state, and calendar policy.
- Training: program and duration, enrollment readiness, trainer qualification/availability/capacity, curriculum/progress evidence, package ledger/session balance, outcome evidence, follow-up trigger and member-facing boundary.
- Retail: product/SKU/category/offering status, stock/on-hand/reserved/available units, customer preference, checkout source, price-adjustment flag, care sensitivity, recommendation rationale/copy, reorder threshold, vendor/catalog relationship.
- Cross-service manager operations: service-demand facts, operating day, location, checkout/retention/data-quality packets, labor-risk/read-model facts, source refs, prior reviewed outcomes, and policy gates.

Scope limits: the packet should identify the location, operating day, customer, pet, reservation, service line, source snapshot, package, inventory, or workflow event. Absence, staleness, ambiguity, or conflicting provider facts should become a review packet or data-quality issue, not an invented answer.

## 5. Agent may draft/recommend/rank/record

Allowed automation verbs are draft/recommend/rank/route/validate/record, not execute:

| Entity/action | May draft, recommend, rank, or record | Source contract |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Boarding capacity | Recommend available/waitlist/deny-with-manager-review evidence for staff; rank capacity exceptions for a manager; draft an internal task explaining source refs. | `domain::boarding::capacity::Policy`, `domain::policy::ReviewGate::ManagerApproval`, `domain::workflow::AllowedAction`. |
| Boarding care/deposit/cancellation/minimum stay | Flag care/medication review, deposit exception, cancellation penalty, minimum-night gap, or holiday/multi-pet buffer; draft staff explanation or review request. | `domain::boarding::{care, deposit, cancellation, minimum_stay}`, `domain::policy::{MedicalDocumentReview, RefundOrDepositException, ManagerApproval}`. |
| Boarding upsell | Recommend an exit-bath opportunity for staff review, suppress when care-sensitive, and draft customer-message copy only behind approval. | `domain::boarding::upsell`, `domain::policy::CustomerMessageApproval`. |
| Daycare coverage and front-desk lane | Rank fast-lane, collection, care-team review, manager-review, waitlist, or policy-block lane from source evidence. | `domain::daycare::{coverage, front_desk}`. |
| Daycare eligibility/assignment/incidents | Recommend group-play eligible/review/ineligible/suspended status, waitlist, behavior review, or incident restriction; draft internal task. | `domain::daycare::{eligibility, assignment, incident}`, `domain::policy::BehaviorReview`. |
| Daycare package opportunity | Recommend staff review for frequent pay-per-visit use, suppress when safety/care/billing evidence is unresolved, and draft approval-required package outreach. | `domain::daycare::package_opportunity`, payment/customer-message gates. |
| Grooming duration/rebooking/reminder | Estimate duration, flag matted/weak-history review, recommend due-now/overdue rebooking, and plan reminder draft with send boundary. | `domain::grooming::{EstimationPolicy, no_show, rebooking, reminder}`. |
| Training assignment/progress/package/follow-up | Draft assignment/waitlist/review decision, progress or outcome summary from evidence, package usage review, and parent follow-up plan. | `domain::training::{availability, progress, outcome, package, follow_up}`. |
| Retail/POS/reorder | Draft internal product recommendation, customer-copy draft for approval, POS review packet, manager/staff reorder task, or vendor notice. | `domain::retail::{pos, recommendation, reorder}`, `storage::service_line::retail`. |
| Manager daily brief | Rank service-demand, capacity, checkout, retention, and source-quality actions; estimate and later record labor minutes, feedback, source refs, and disposition. | `app::manager_daily_brief::{Packet, BriefAction, SafeAgentAction, OutcomeRecord}`, `storage::operations::ManagerDailyBriefOutcomeRecord`. |

When workflow policy exposes `domain::policy::automation::Level`, describe the action as draft-only, internal-task-only, manager-approval-required, or never-automate according to the source value. Do not upgrade authority in prose.

## 6. Agent must not do directly

| Blocked direct action | Why blocked | Correct safe path |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Confirm, reject, cancel, check in/out, waitlist-release, or close a reservation/stay. | Reservation lifecycle and customer commitment require source-system authority and staff/manager review. | Draft staff packet or `RequestHumanReview`; staff/provider workflow executes separately. |
| Promise availability, allocate rooms, change room/playgroup/groomer/trainer assignment, or mutate capacity/schedules. | Capacity and assignments affect pet safety, labor, customer trust, and provider inventory. | Route to front-desk lead, care/daycare lead, groomer/trainer, or manager with source refs and review gate. |
| Change staff schedule, labor roster, payroll, or final labor assignment. | Personnel/labor actions require manager or HR/ops-approved workflow. | Manager Daily Brief may rank demand/staffing risk and record outcome; manager executes elsewhere. |
| Send, queue, suppress, or publish customer/member/public messages. | Recipient, channel, timing, consent, sensitive facts, and wording need approval. | Draft message body and route to customer-message reviewer/approved sender. |
| Mutate Gingr/PMS/POS/provider records, create/delete/merge records, hide source issues, or edit material audit history. | Provider/source authority and audit integrity remain outside agent control. | Create data-quality/internal task or provider-portal review packet; IT/security/product approves any write-back mode. |
| Collect/capture/retry/void/refund payments, waive/forfeit deposits, apply discounts/credits, alter rates/taxes/fees, or attach retail products to checkout. | Money movement and POS/ledger changes require payment/accounting/manager approval. | Draft payment/accounting review packet with source refs and `RefundOrDepositException` where relevant. |
| Approve vaccine, medical, medication, behavior, group-play, incident, safety, or local policy exceptions. | Pet safety and local policy require trained reviewer, daycare/behavior lead, medical/vaccine staff, or manager. | Route to `MedicalDocumentReview`, `BehaviorReview`, or `ManagerApproval` with uncertainty preserved. |
| Consume package sessions, enroll packages, mark graduation/outcome claims final, or reinstate incident-restricted pets. | Package balances, training outcomes, and safety restrictions require evidence and staff approval. | Draft package ledger/reconciliation, trainer evidence packet, or behavior/manager review. |
| Place vendor orders or treat retail recommendations as medical/care promises. | Vendor spend, inventory policy, and medical-claim language need manager/vendor/customer-message review. | Draft staff task, manager review, or customer-safe copy for approval. |
| Destructive cleanup, broad document/PII exposure, policy changes, secret-dependent or live external side effects. | Security, privacy, compliance, and production-safety boundaries. | Fail closed, create internal task, and ask product/ops/IT/security owner for approval evidence. |

## 7. Required human reviewer role(s) and approval condition

| Role | Usually approves for this family | Does not approve |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Staff/front-desk lead | Routine intake completeness, boarding/daycare/grooming/training/retail queue handoff quality, source-backed internal tasks, package/rebooking draft readiness when no sensitive gate exists. | Manager exceptions, money movement, provider write-back, medical/behavior approval, final customer send outside approved sender role. |
| General manager / assistant general manager | Capacity overrides, staffing/labor risk, schedule-impacting exceptions, minimum-stay/cancellation exceptions, incident restrictions, suppression/escalation, customer-trust decisions, manager brief disposition. Maps to `ReviewGate::ManagerApproval`. | Medical/vaccine validity unless trained, payment processing unless assigned, IT/security integration scope, unrelated policy changes. |
| Daycare/behavior lead or trained care-team reviewer | Group-play eligibility, temperament review, playgroup safety, daycare incident restrictions, assignment safety. Maps to `ReviewGate::BehaviorReview` when behavior/group-play evidence is unresolved. | Payment, provider write-back, broad policy or customer-message approval outside safety wording. |
| Medical/vaccine qualified staff | Vaccine/medical/care-document ambiguity, medication/care readiness, medical-document review for boarding/daycare/training/grooming sensitivity. Maps to `ReviewGate::MedicalDocumentReview`. | Money movement, provider integration permissions, final customer send except medical wording review. |
| Groomer/trainer / service-line specialist | Grooming duration/history/style review, named-groomer or named-trainer suitability, training progress/outcome evidence, package/session reality. | Final calendar/provider mutation, payment adjustments, broad manager exceptions, customer send without approved path. |
| Customer-message reviewer / approved sender | Final recipient, channel, timing, body, contact preference/consent, suppression/send decision for upsell, rebooking, reminder, package, progress, retail, or service-line outreach. Maps to `ReviewGate::CustomerMessageApproval`. | Provider/PMS/POS mutation, payment movement, medical/behavior approval, schedule/capacity execution. |
| Payment/accounting reviewer | Deposits, refunds, waivers, forfeits, discounts, balances, POS/payment ambiguity, package billing mismatch. Maps to `ReviewGate::RefundOrDepositException` when appropriate. | Medical/behavior/schedule decisions, non-payment policy, customer-message tone except payment facts. |
| IT/security | Integration scope, provider write-back mode, secrets, logging, rate limits, broad access, and tool-port failure modes. | Business policy approval and individual live action approval unless also named operational owner. |
| Product/ops owner | Whether a workflow, port, or new service-line automation is allowed to exist and under what policy. | Individual live execution without the correct operational reviewer and source-system approval. |

| Recommendation or draft | Required source evidence first | If missing/stale/ambiguous |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Boarding care/check-in readiness | Pet care profile, feeding instructions, medication review requirement, medical/care document state, reservation context, source refs. | Route to medical/care review; do not clear check-in or make medical judgment. |
| Boarding deposit/cancellation/minimum-stay answer | Deposit rule/payment timing, deposit/payment reference, notice hours, stay nights, holiday/multi-pet/local policy reason, source refs. | Route to payment/accounting or manager; do not waive/forfeit/refund/shorten stay. |
| Daycare group-play or assignment | Pet species, spay/neuter, temperament assessment freshness, vaccine readiness, incident restrictions, staff coverage, playgroup capacity, contract rules. | Route to behavior/daycare lead, medical/vaccine review, or manager; do not assign playgroup/admit. |
| Daycare package opportunity | Attendance visit count, package state, payment/billing state, care eligibility, customer-message readiness, source refs. | Suppress or route to billing/care/customer-message review; do not enroll/sell package. |
| Grooming duration/rebooking/reminder | Service type, breed/coat facts, prior approved grooming history, care/style references, no-show history, cadence, consent/approval state, availability evidence. | Route to groomer/manager/customer-message review; do not book slot, discount, charge deposit, or send reminder. |
| Training assignment/progress/outcome/follow-up | Program, trainer requirement/availability/capacity, behavior/care readiness, package ledger/session balance, progress evidence, outcome claims, member-facing boundary. | Route to trainer/manager/behavior/payment/customer-message review; do not assign trainer, consume sessions, or send parent update. |
| Retail recommendation/POS/reorder | Product/SKU/category/offering status, inventory position, checkout source, price adjustment, customer preference, care sensitivity, vendor relationship, source refs. | Route to staff/manager/payment/customer-message/vendor review; do not attach product, place order, or claim medical benefit. |
| Manager Daily Brief service-line action | Service-demand/read-model fact, operating day/location/service line, source refs, labor threshold, checkout/retention/data-quality packet where relevant. | Mark wrong-source or data-quality issue; do not count value or change schedule/capacity. |

## 9. Outcome/audit record proving safe use and value measurement

Safe use requires proof of each stage, not just source evidence or draft creation.

| Proof needed | Example field or record | What it proves | What it does not prove |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Source evidence | `domain::source::RecordRef`, `Provenance`, `StoredSourceRecordRef`, projection version, payload hash, source snapshot id. | The recommendation was grounded in a named source fact. | Approval, execution, or correctness when facts are stale/wrong. |
| Draft/recommendation | `app::manager_daily_brief::Packet`, `BriefAction`, safe action list, blocked action list, internal task draft, message draft id/body ref, review gate. | Work product was prepared and constrained. | Customer/provider/payment/schedule action happened. |
| Human approval | Approval record, reviewer/actor/persona, `ReviewGate`, decision reason, timestamp, status. | The sensitive step was reviewed by the named role. | Permission for unrelated steps or future automation expansion. |
| Blocked-action rejection | blocked action reason, requested side effects, validator result, `live_side_effects_allowed: false`, `outcome_persisted: false` where applicable. | Unsafe requested side effects were not executed or persisted. | That the underlying operational problem was solved. |
| Outcome/value | `app::manager_daily_brief::OutcomeRecord`; `storage::operations::ManagerDailyBriefOutcomeRecord` fields such as action id, outcome, before minutes, actual minutes, actor/persona, feedback, source refs, correlation id, location, operating day, action kind, owner persona, estimated minutes saved. | Reviewed work, manager/staff disposition, wrong-source finding, and measured handle-time/minutes delta. | Guaranteed future ROI or proof that an agent performed live service-line execution. |

Value measurement for this family should use one or more of: minutes avoided in capacity/package/service-line review, rework reduced from wrong-source findings, handle time reduced for front desk/manager queues, manager/staff disposition, before-vs-actual minutes, source-fact-was-wrong outcomes, suppressed/blocked unsafe actions, and outcome capture tied to source refs and correlation id.

- The opening names front-desk, service-line, manager, groomer/trainer, and regional labor reduced.
